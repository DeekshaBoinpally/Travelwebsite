Munnar is one of the most popular hill stations in Kerala, known for its cool climate and natural beauty. It is famous for vast tea plantations, misty hills, and scenic valleys. Munnar is an ideal destination for nature lovers and peaceful holidays.

Alleppey is one of the most beautiful destinations in Kerala, known for its backwaters and houseboats. The calm canals, coconut palms, and village scenery make it a perfect place to relax. It is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Kerala.

Padmanabha Swamy Temple is one of the most famous temples in Kerala. It is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is known for its spiritual importance. The temple features traditional Dravidian architecture and attracts devotees from all over the world.
