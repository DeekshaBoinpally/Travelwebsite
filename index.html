Most famous places

Kerala

Kerala is famous for its lush greenery, backwaters, and peaceful beaches. It offers a unique blend of nature, culture, and Ayurveda traditions. Alleppey, Munnar, and Kochi attract visitors from around the world.

Himachal pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is known for its snow-covered mountains and scenic valleys. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Shimla, Manali, and Dharamshala are popular tourist places.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is known for its royal palaces, forts, and rich heritage. It showcases vibrant culture, traditional festivals, and desert landscapes. Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer are its most famous tourist destinations.
